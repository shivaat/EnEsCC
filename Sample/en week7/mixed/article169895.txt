Singapore Airlines jet 'faceplants' as landing gear retracts 
Updated 0357 GMT (1057 HKT) October 12, 2015 
Despite a recent landing gear mishap, Singapore Airlines has an excellent safety record. 
Story highlights 
Singapore Airlines Airbus damaged as plane's landing gear retracts during a test 
One engineer was on board but was not injured 
(CNN) 
A Singapore Airlines jet faceplanted when its forward landing gear retracted on the tarmac at Singapore's Changi Airport Sunday. 
The Airbus A330-300 was undergoing a landing gear check when the incident occurred, according to a Singapore Airlines statement.
